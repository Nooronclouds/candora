# Candora Financial Report Q1 2024

**Effective Date:** 2024-04-15

## Executive Summary
This report summarizes the financial performance of Candora for the first quarter ending March 31, 2024. Overall performance exceeded targets due to robust growth in SaaS subscriptions and enterprise licensing.

## Revenue and Income
During Q1 2024, the company achieved the following financial metrics:

| Metric | Amount | Growth YoY |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Revenue | $12.5 Million | +14.2% |
| Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) | $3.2 Million | +8.5% |
| Gross Profit | $9.3 Million | +16.3% |
| Operating Expenses | $6.5 Million | +11.2% |
| Net Income | $1.8 Million | +18.4% |

## Operating Metrics
- **SaaS ARR:** $42.0 Million at quarter-end.
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR):** 112%.
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Payback:** 11 months.
