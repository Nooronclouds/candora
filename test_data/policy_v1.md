# Employee Handbook and Policies v1.0

**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01  
**Policy ID:** HR-2024-001  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Classification:** Internal Policy  

## Section 1: Introduction and Scope
This document outlines the standard operating policies, benefits, and workplace guidelines for all full-time employees.

## Section 2: Remote Work Eligibility
Candora values workplace flexibility. 
- All full-time employees are eligible for remote work.
- Employees are required to work from the main office at least 4 days per week (allowing for 1 day of remote work).
- Core hours in the office are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

## Section 3: Annual Leave and Vacation Policy
All full-time employees receive paid time off to support work-life balance.
- Employees receive 15 days of paid annual vacation vacation days.
- Vacation requests must be submitted at least two weeks in advance.
- Unused vacation days up to 5 days can be rolled over to the next calendar year.

## Section 4: Travel and Expense Reimbursement
All business travel expenses must align with company budget parameters.
- The pre-approval threshold for business travel expenses is $500. Any single expense item above $500 requires direct VP approval prior to purchase.
- Receipts must be submitted within 30 days of the travel event.

## Section 5: Maternity and Parental Leave
Full-time employees are eligible for parental benefits after 6 months of continuous employment.
- Full-time employees receive 12 weeks of fully paid maternity leave.
- Partners are eligible for 2 weeks of paid parental leave.
