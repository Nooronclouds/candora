# Benefits and Remote Work Policy Addendum v1.1

**Effective Date:** 2024-07-01  
**Policy ID:** HR-2024-001-ADD  
**Version:** 1.1  
**Classification:** Internal Policy Addendum  

## Section 1: Purpose of Addendum
This document serves as an official addendum modifying the policy terms set forth in the Employee Handbook v1.0. Where conflicts exist, this addendum takes precedence for dates following July 1, 2024.

## Section 2: Updated Remote Work Guidelines
Due to positive feedback and productivity gains, remote work limits have been revised.
- Employees are required to work from the office only 2 days per week (allowing for 3 days of remote work).
- Core in-office days are Tuesday and Thursday.

## Section 3: Vacation Policy Update
Annual paid time off is increased to improve employee retention and satisfaction.
- All full-time employees are now allowed 20 days of paid annual vacation vacation days.
- Rollover terms remain unchanged.

## Section 4: Travel and Expense Reimbursement Adjustments
In order to manage operational costs, travel expense policies have been tightened.
- The pre-approval threshold for travel expenses is $250. Any single expense item above $250 requires VP pre-approval.
- Booking must be completed through the company's approved portal.
